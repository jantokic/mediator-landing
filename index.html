Features

Everything you need for collective AI reasoning

Structured debates with convergence detection, built-in personas, and real-time summaries — all from your menu bar.

Agent-to-Agent Debate

Two AI personas autonomously discuss any topic with structured turns, real arguments, and a neutral convergence judge. No prompt engineering — just state the question.

Auto Convergence

Independent LLM judge scores agreement after each round. When agents align, the debate ends.

Built-in Personas

Four thinking styles, ready out of the box.

Pragmatist Visionary Devil's Advocate Technical Lead

Structured Summaries

Every debate produces actionable output: points of agreement, disagreement, strongest arguments, and a clear verdict with recommended next steps. No more parsing raw chat logs.

Menu Bar Native

Lives in your macOS menu bar. One click, no browser tabs, no context switching.
